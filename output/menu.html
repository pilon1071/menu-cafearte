El Espresso es la esencia pura del café: intenso, aromático y lleno de carácter. Su historia nace en Italia a finales del siglo XIX y principios del siglo XX, cuando se buscaba una forma más rápida de preparar café sin perder calidad ni sabor. Con el tiempo, el espresso se convirtió en la base de muchas bebidas clásicas como el cappuccino, latte, americano, cortado y flat white. Perfecto para quienes disfrutan un café fuerte, elegante y auténtico, el Espresso es ideal para comenzar el día, cerrar una comida o disfrutar una pausa breve con todo el sabor del café en su máxima expresión.

Espresso is the pure essence of coffee: intense, aromatic and full of character. It is prepared by passing hot water under pressure through finely ground coffee, creating a small but powerful beverage, with deep body, concentrated flavor and a delicate layer of golden crema on the surface. Its history begins in Italy at the end of the nineteenth and beginning of the twentieth century, when a faster way to prepare coffee was sought without losing quality or flavor. Hence its name, related to the idea of preparing coffee "on the spot" or "expressly" for each person. Over time, espresso became the base for many classic beverages such as cappuccino, latte, americano, cortado and flat white. Perfect for those who enjoy strong, elegant and authentic coffee, Espresso is ideal for starting the day, ending a meal or enjoying a brief break with all the flavor of coffee at its finest.
